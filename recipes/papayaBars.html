Papaya Bars

Description

Papaya Bars are a silky smooth custard using fresh papaya fruit on a bed of short bread crust. Pack these bars up with a Grinder Sandwich and this Italian Pasta Salad for the perfect picnic.

Ingredients

  • Papaya puree (Red Papaya)
  • Honey
  • Lemon Juice

Instructions

  1. Make the crust.
  2. Bake the crust.
  3. Make the filling.
  4. Bake the bars until set.
  5. Dust the top with powdered sugar
