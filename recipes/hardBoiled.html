Hard Boiled Eggs

Description

Hard boiled eggs are great food to have on hand as their uses are so versatile. Not only are they super delicious on their own, but theyâ€™re great in sandwiches, chopped up on salads and the foundation for all devilled eggs. The trick to great hard boiled eggs is not over-cooking them, which can leave a grey ring around the yolk and make their texture a bit rubbery.

Ingredients

  • A pot with lid
  • Eggs
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Place your eggs in a single layer on the bottom of your pot and cover with cold water.
  2. Bring your eggs to a rolling boil.
  3. Remove from heat and let stand in water for 10-12 minutes.
  4. Drain water and immediately run cold water over eggs.
