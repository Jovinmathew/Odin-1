Healthy Homemade Vegimite Alternative

Description

Vegimite aka salted Nukes.

Ingredients

  • Black tahini
  • Coconut Aminos
  • Brewers yeast
  • Malt extract syrup
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Good Salt

Instructions

Add all ingredients to blender and blend until smooth and creamy. Place in a jar. Store in the fridge for up to 18 months. The more salt you use, the longer it will last
